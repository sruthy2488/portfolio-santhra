About Me

I completed my MBBS at the prestigious Nicolae Testemitanu State University of Medicine and Pharmacy in Moldova, where I learned that saving lives requires both skill and a sense of humor—because laughter is the best medicine, after all!

When I’m not diagnosing or prescribing, you’ll find me reading up on the latest medical trends, trying to master new languages (Moldovan was just the start), or debating with my friends about whether pineapple belongs on pizza.

I’m committed to delivering top-notch healthcare and constantly improving my knowledge to make sure I’m providing the best care possible.

So, if you’re in need of medical advice—or just a good conversation—I’m your go-to!
